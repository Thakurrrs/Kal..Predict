## Change Types
- Standard change
- High-risk change
- Emergency change

## PR and Review Rules
- Required reviewers: founder self-review at minimum; external review recommended for high-risk changes.
- Checklist requirements: scope, risk impact, rollback plan, linked docs/ADR updates.
- Test evidence requirements: relevant test outputs and benchmark deltas attached.
- Security sign-off requirements: mandatory for auth, execution, and secret-handling changes.

## Release Sign-Off
- Product sign-off: founder approval against PRD acceptance criteria.
- Engineering sign-off: founder verification of technical readiness.
- Risk/Security sign-off: founder verification of controls and unresolved risk status.
- Operations readiness: runbook and incident readiness confirmed.

## Emergency Change Rule
Emergency changes are allowed only for containment or safety, and must be followed by post-change documentation and formal retrospective within the next review cycle.
