## Severity Levels
- SEV-1: Critical trading or security impact
- SEV-2: Major service degradation
- SEV-3: Minor or localized issue

## Response Flow
1. Detect and classify incident.
2. Trigger containment actions (including kill switch if needed).
3. Communicate status and owners.
4. Restore service safely.
5. Complete post-incident review.

## Communication Templates
- Internal update template: issue summary, severity, impacted components, containment status, next checkpoint.
- Stakeholder update template: plain-language impact summary, current safeguards, expected next update.
- Closure summary template: root cause, timeline, containment, corrective actions, prevention actions.

## Incident Closure Criteria
- Immediate risk is contained.
- Root cause is documented.
- Corrective actions are tracked with owners.
- Related runbooks and controls are updated.
